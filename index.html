Introduction to AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. It has become a key technology in various fields, from healthcare to entertainment, and is revolutionizing how we interact with technology.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a subset of AI that focuses on the development of systems that can learn and improve automatically from experience without being explicitly programmed. It powers technologies such as recommendation systems, image recognition, and autonomous vehicles.

Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing (NLP) enables machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. It is the driving force behind voice assistants like Siri and Alexa, as well as translation tools and chatbots.

AI in Robotics

AI-powered robotics combines artificial intelligence with physical robots to perform complex tasks. From manufacturing and logistics to space exploration, robots equipped with AI are enhancing efficiency and capabilities.
